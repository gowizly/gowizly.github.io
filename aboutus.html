About Wizly

We're on a mission to simplify family life through intelligent technology, helping parents stay organized and never miss what matters most.

Our Story

Born from Real Family Challenges

Wizly was founded in 2023 when our team of working parents realized we were all struggling with the same overwhelming challenge: keeping track of our children's increasingly complex schedules while managing our own busy lives.

Between school emails, assignment due dates, extracurricular activities, and family commitments, important things were falling through the cracks. We knew there had to be a better way.

That's when we decided to combine our expertise in AI technology with our understanding of family dynamics to create something truly helpful for modern parents.
