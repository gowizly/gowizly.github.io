Company Overview

Wizly is a leading family calendar management platform that uses AI technology to help parents organize their children's schedules, assignments, and events. Founded in 2023 by working parents who understood the challenges of modern family life, Wizly has grown to serve over 50,000 families worldwide.

Our mission is to reduce family stress through intelligent automation, allowing parents to focus on what truly matters: spending quality time with their children.
